Signs of Stress


When a person has long-term (chronic) stress, continued activation of the stress response causes wear and tear on the body. Physical, emotional and behavioral symptoms develop.


1. Physical symptoms:


路 Aches and pains, such as chest pain or a feeling like your heart is racing

路 Exhaustion or trouble sleeping

路 Headaches, dizziness or shaking

路 High blood pressure

路 Muscle tension or jaw clenching

路 Stomach or digestive problems

路 Weak immune system



2. Emotional and mental symptoms:


路 Sadness

路 Panic attacks

路 Anxiety or irritability

路 Depression
